# Minimalism & Negative Space

In Whisper's design, **Negative Space** is not just emptiness; it is a **meaningful silence**.

### Core Principles
1. **Reduce Noise**: Remove every non-essential decoration.
2. **Focus & Hierarchy**: Use white space to guide the eye precisely.
3. **Breathability**: Allow components to exist in a state of grace without crowding.

> "Less is more, but only if the 'less' is precise."
